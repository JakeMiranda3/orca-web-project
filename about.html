My Mission

My mission is to protect orca populations through education and community action. It's a growing concern that the orca population has been on a steady decline due to numerous factors mainly from human actions. These problems include pollution, climate change, whaling, and holding them in captivity. My goals include raising awareness about the threats orca face, highlighting the decline in their population, and showing ways you can get involved. I urge you to take five minutes out of your day to explore this website or sign a petition. Every small action can help protect these marine mammals.
